Parameters
Split ratio 2.0
How many PRs one logical change is divided into. 1 = no splitting.
Attribution prob. 0.30
Fraction of incidents traced back to the split strategy.
Policy strength 0.20
How aggressively the team responds when the pattern is detected.
Trust erosion rate 0.10
How fast trust in metrics collapses per attributed incident.
Norm. amplifier 1.50
How much normalization multiplies the effective split rate.
